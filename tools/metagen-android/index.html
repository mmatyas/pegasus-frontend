Unlike on Windows or Linux, apps on Android are launched in a completely different way. Futhermore, popular emulators like RetroArch may require more complex launch commands and additional parameters to work. To help with setting things up, this page can generate base collections for Pegasus metadata files for various known emulators.

(NOTE: You can find standalone emulators after RetroArch.)
